<b>Q-Wiki</b>
<i>A strategic Wikidata based knowledge game</i>

This project was developed over the course of two semesters (April 2019 - February 2020).
It is a cooperation between the University of Applied Sciences Berlin (HTW Berlin) and Wikimedia Germany.
